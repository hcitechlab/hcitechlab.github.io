About Internship [Apply Now]

I started as an assistant professor in the Graduate School of Culture Technology at KAIST in September 2021 and launched HCI Tech Lab.
We are looking for a few undergraduate research interns for 2022 Spring Semester.

HCI Tech Lab Introduction

As an interdisciplinary research group, we build physical/digital interactive system empowered by novel sensing/haptic technologies. In HCI Tech Lab, we aim to explore novel interactive technologies that bring direct benefits to real world users. Our research process generally includes:

  • Find gaps between interface/device and human
  • Bridge the gap with novel technical/social solution
  • Evaluate the solution with research methods
  • Deploy the solution with practical applications
Refer to recent papers for examples.
